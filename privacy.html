Privacy Policy

USFS Photo Collector

Effective date: May 1, 2026

USFS Photo Collector ("the App") is a field-data collection tool published by HGS Engineering for documenting site observations on U.S. Forest Service lands. This policy describes what information the App handles and how.

What the App collects

The App captures the following on your device when you use it:

  • Photos you take with the in-app camera control.
  • GPS coordinates when you tap the GPS button or take a photo with location services enabled.
  • Text you enter, including descriptions, location names, region/forest selections, and protocol-area selections.

Where your data is stored

All photos, coordinates, and text you enter are stored locally on your device, in your browser's storage (localStorage / IndexedDB). Nothing is uploaded automatically. The App has no user accounts, no analytics, and no third-party tracking SDKs.

Data leaves your device only when you explicitly export or back up using the in-app Export or Backup buttons. At that point the export file goes wherever you direct it (Files app, email attachment, AirDrop, etc.) — the App itself does not transmit it to any server.

Permissions the App requests

  • Camera — to take photos directly inside the App.
  • Location (GPS) — to tag photos and entries with coordinates.

You may decline either permission; affected features will be disabled but the rest of the App continues to work.

Third parties

The App does not share data with any third party. It does not contain advertising, analytics, or social-media SDKs. The App's static files are served from Microsoft Azure Static Web Apps; Microsoft may log connection-level information (IP address, request time) per their standard hosting practice. The App does not send user-entered content to Microsoft or any other vendor.

Children's data

The App is intended for U.S. Forest Service personnel and contractors. It is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

Your control over your data

Because all data is local to your device, you can delete it at any time by clearing the App's storage in your device settings, or by removing the App.

Changes to this policy

Material changes will be reflected on this page along with an updated effective date.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to HGS Engineering. The project's source repository is at github.com/whittw1/USFS-Photo-Tool.
